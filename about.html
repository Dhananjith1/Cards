About eColink

eColink Smart Card is an innovative NFC-enabled digital business card made using eco-friendly materials. It empowers professionals and businesses to share key information instantly while choosing a smarter, paperless networking approach.

Our Vision

To transform traditional networking into a modern, digital-first experience that is fast, memorable, and environmentally responsible.

Our Mission

To provide premium NFC smart cards that help users instantly connect, showcase their brand, and reduce dependence on disposable printed cards.

How eColink Works

A simple three-step process designed for business events, meetings, and everyday introductions.

1. Customize Your Card

Choose your package, add your logo and brand identity, and build your digital profile with links, contact details, and business information.

2. Tap to Share

During networking, your eColink card instantly opens your digital profile on compatible devices. No app download is needed for the recipient.

3. Build Lasting Connections

Recipients can save your details immediately, follow your social channels, and engage with your brand right after a single interaction.
